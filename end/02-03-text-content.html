The Perfect Baked Potato Recipe

Description

Today I’m going to share with you my great great grandmother’s recipe for a baked potato.

Now, you might be thinking to yourself… “it’s a baked potato, how hard could it be.” But it wasn’t until Easter last year when I was preparing dinner with my own mother when I realized how much better a plain baked potato could be.

Whether you realize it or not, many factors contribute to how rich and buttery your baked potatoes taste. And there are a multitude of toppings that can take you from zero to 100 real quick! Today I’ll share with you my family secrets so you can wow your own family this holiday season.

Ingredients

  • 5 Russet potatoes
  • 5 tbsp butter, cut into one tbsp pieces
  • Coarse sea salt
  • 5 strips of bacon, cooked and chopped
  • Chives to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash potatoes and pierce all over with a fork
  2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Bake potatoes in oven for 25 minutes
  4. Once cooked, top with butter, bacon, chives and sea salt
